I have absolutely no desire to read a political book at this time, so I have no plans to read it. I think the cover speaks for itself. America was once a great fearless nation and now sometimes I think are scared of our own shadow. The rugged characters that built this nation would be sickened to see the fearful weak people whoÂ inherited its wealth. We watch the news to be informed, but all we get are stories to make us more fearful. Then we seek out the opinions of _experts_, because we are afraid to trust our instincts and make our own decisions. Then the _experts_ are wrong and we get more fearful, so we seek out new _experts_. What should you do about it? Ask yourself.
