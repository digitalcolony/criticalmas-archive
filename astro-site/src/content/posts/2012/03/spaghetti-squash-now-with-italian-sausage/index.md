I've long since given up regular spaghetti. These days I use the spaghetti squash. I detailed my pasta journey in the post [Spaghetti Evolution The Road to Paleolithic Pasta](/2010/11/spaghetti-evolution-the-road-to-paleolitic-pasta/). This week I was inspired by a recipe onlineÂ to swap out the ground beef for Italian Sausage. It turned out great, both as dinner and for leftovers the next day.

![spaghetti-squash](./img/spaghetti-squash.jpg)

_Spaghetti Squash and Italian Sausage_

The only variation I did with the recipe is I now cut the spaghetti squash and scoop out the inner guts prior to baking. I found you can reduce the oven time down from an hour to 35-40 minutes. The downside to the fast method is I think it makes the result a little more watery. I'll keep experimenting.
